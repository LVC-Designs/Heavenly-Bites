Welcome to Heavenly Bites

Hello there!

We're Tonia and Anthony, the hearts and hands behind Heavenly Bites. As parents with a passion for baking, we've turned our home kitchen into a haven of heavenly aromas and delightful treats.

At Heavenly Bites, we believe that the secret ingredient in every recipe is:

love

Each cookie, cake, and pastry is crafted from scratch, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

These cherished formulas, combined with a dash of modern creativity, result in treats that are both nostalgic and exciting.

What started as a way to share our love for baking with family and friends has blossomed into a home bakery that's been delighting taste buds across our community.

You might have seen us at local farmer's markets, charity bake sales, or community festivals - we love nothing more than seeing the joy our baked goods bring to people's faces!

Every batch that comes out of our oven is infused with the same care and attention we put into baking for our own children. From classic chocolate chip cookies that take you back to your childhood, to innovative flavor combinations that surprise and delight, each item is a labor of love.

Thank you for visiting Heavenly Bites.
We can't wait to bake something special just for you!





About Us

At Heavenly Bites, we believe that every bite should be a little piece of heaven. Founded by Tonia and Anthony, a dynamic duo of passionate home bakers, our journey began in the heart of our family kitchen. With a shared love for baking and a commitment to quality, we've turned our passion into a beloved home bakery that brings joy to our community.

Our story is simple yet heartfelt. As parents, we wanted to create something special for our children—treats that not only tasted great but also carried the warmth of home. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a full-fledged business, fueled by the smiles of our family, friends, and neighbors who couldn't get enough of our baked goods.

Every treat that comes out of our oven is made with love, using the finest ingredients and time-tested recipes handed down through generations. Whether it's a classic favorite like our signature chocolate chip cookies or a new, inventive flavor that pushes the boundaries of tradition, we pour our hearts into every batch.

But Heavenly Bites is more than just a bakery—it's a reflection of our values. We believe in the power of community, and you'll often find us at local farmer's markets, charity events, and community gatherings, sharing our creations and spreading joy one sweet treat at a time.

Tonia's creative flair and Anthony's meticulous attention to detail ensure that every order is crafted to perfection. Together, we bring a blend of tradition and innovation to every product, creating a unique experience that our customers have come to love.

We’re grateful for the support of our community and are excited to continue sharing our passion for baking with all of you. Whether you're looking for a sweet treat for yourself or a special order for a celebration, we're here to make your day a little sweeter.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We can’t wait to share our love for baking with you.





Our Menu

Savory Foccacia


Our Savory Focaccia is a culinary treasure, crafted from a cherished family recipe that has been passed down through generations. Baked to golden perfection, this focaccia is infused with aromatic herbs and drizzled with olive oil, creating a delightful harmony of flavors. Every bite delivers a crispy crust and a tender, fluffy interior, making it a true comfort food, lovingly made from scratch.
Everything made from scratch and with love.

Cinnamon Rolls


Indulge in our Cinnamon Rolls, a personal favorite that never fails to delight. These soft, pillowy buns are swirled with the perfect amount of cinnamon and topped with a luscious, creamy glaze. The rich, aromatic filling melds seamlessly with the tender dough, creating a mouthwatering experience that is both sweet and satisfying.

Chocolate Chip Banana Bread


Our Chocolate Chip Banana Bread is a delicious twist on a classic treat. Moist and flavorful, this bread is packed with ripe bananas and dotted with rich chocolate chips that melt into the dough as it bakes. The result is a perfect blend of sweetness and indulgence, with every slice offering a delightful contrast between the dense banana bread and the gooey chocolate pockets.

Chocolate Chip Cookies


Our Chocolate Chip Cookies are the ultimate comfort food, baked to perfection with a golden exterior and a soft, chewy center. Each cookie is loaded with rich, semi-sweet chocolate chips that melt in your mouth, making them an irresistible treat. Whether you enjoy them warm from the oven or as a midday snack, these cookies are a classic favorite for all ages.

Butter Tarts


Experience the indulgence of our Butter Tarts, a quintessentially Canadian treat that’s as rich as it is satisfying. Our tarts feature a flaky, buttery pastry shell filled with a luscious, gooey center that is the perfect balance of sweet and buttery goodness. Whether you prefer them with raisins or without, these tarts are a decadent delight that will have you coming back for more.
